What makes WebRTC special is that the data travels from one client to another without going through the server. \r\n\r\nThe main functions of WebRTC can be broadly categorized into three types. \r\n\r\n- Access and acquire video and audio streams\r\n- Establish a connection between peers and stream audio/video.\r\n- Communicate arbitrary data.\r\n\r\nWebRTC uses three different JavaScript APIs to perform these three functions.
