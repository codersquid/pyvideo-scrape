            "description": "As scientific datasets continue to grow exponentially in size, the resource requirements of even simple analyses can quickly grow to become a problem -- e.g. the job takes an unreasonably long time, or simply runs out of space.\r\n\r\nExisting solutions to this may be powerful, but can also come with a large complexity overhead, especially for the non-expert user.
