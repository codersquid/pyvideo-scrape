The problem is that the indexing mechanisms for patents and\npublications are not as good as they could be, making good prior art searching\nmore of an art than a science. This is done in two ways - by modeling each patent as a document vector and a graph node. \n  * Part IIA (7 mins): Patents as document vectors.* Once we have a patent as a data structure, we can treat the patent as a vector in an n-dimensional space.
